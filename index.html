The Stroop Effect Experiment

Welcome to this interactive Stroop test

  • 10 words will be shown in each test.
  • There will be a total of 10 tests.
  • There will be no timer between words.
  • Each word will be displayed in a color.
  • Your task is to press the key that corresponds to the color of the word, not what the word says.
  • Use the following keys: A (Red), S (Green), D (Blue), F (Yellow).
  • There will be a short timer between each test to prepare you for the next round.

Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible. Once the test is completed, you can download your reaction time data.
